Saab 9-3 Cup Holder Insert (2003-11)

This insert takes place of the stock "Smart Slot" found on 2003-2011 Saab 9-3's. Rather than using the complex (and often fragile) design originally offered by Saab, this cupholder is created as one piece with no moving parts.

This part uses the existing screws (not included) that held on the original insert.

* Please read the description of each material type (found below) before purchasing.

** FREE standard shipping to continental US. International shipping is not currently offered.

Material Options

* Inserts can be 3D printed in either PLA or PETG at this time. Details on each material can be found below:
PLA:

PLA is the most common plastic used for 3D printing. It is relatively strong, durable, and affordable. PLA MAY WARP IN EXTREME TEMPERATURES. For cold climates, this should not be an issue. However, if you tend to leave your car out in the sun (especially in warmer climates) there is a risk that your cupholder may warp, sag, or otherwise fail over time. I would not reccommend leaving a drink unattended in a hot car at the risk of the cupholder failing and spilling into the ignition.

PETG:

PETG is slightly stronger than PLA, but can withstand higher temperatures without warping. This material is highly reccomended to ensure that your cupholder functions and lasts as long as possible. Components made in PETG can be left in the sun for extended periods of time without any issue.
