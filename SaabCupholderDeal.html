3D Printed Saab 9-3 Cup Holder Insert (2003-11)

This insert takes place of the stock "Smart Slot" found on 2003-2011 Saab 9-3's. Rather than using the complex (and often fragile) design originally offered by Saab, this cupholder is 3D Printed as one piece with no moving parts.

This part uses the existing screws (not included) that held on the original insert.
